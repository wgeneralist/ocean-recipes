Tuna Poke Bowl

Description

A fresh, vibrant dish thatâ€™s perfect for a light, healthy meal.

Ingridents

  • 1 lb sushi-grade tuna (cubed)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp honey or agave
  • 1/4 cup cucumber (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup avocado (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup edamame (optional)
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup cooked sushi rice
  • Seaweed (for garnish)
  • Sesame seeds

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix together the soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, and honey/agave. Add the cubed tuna and toss to coat. Let it marinate for 10 minutes.
  2. While the tuna marinates, prepare the rice and place it in the bottom of a bowl.
  3. Arrange the cucumber, avocado, shredded carrots, and edamame around the rice.
  4. Top with the marinated tuna and drizzle with a bit more soy sauce.
  5. Garnish with sesame seeds and seaweed.
