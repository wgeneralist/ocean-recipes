Grilled Salmon Lemon Dill Sauce

Description

A classic and simple way to prepare salmon, packed with flavor.

Ingridents

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 lemon (sliced)
  • Fresh dill (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill or grill pan on medium-high heat.
  2. Rub salmon fillets with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the fillets on the grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, until the fish is cooked through.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together the sour cream, Dijon mustard, fresh dill, and a squeeze of lemon juice.
  5. Serve the salmon with a dollop of the lemon dill sauce and garnish with extra lemon slices.
