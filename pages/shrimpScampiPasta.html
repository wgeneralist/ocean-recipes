Shrimp Scampi Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 8 oz linguine or spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh parsley (chopped)
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until pink and opaque. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the butter and garlic. Saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the white wine and chicken broth, scraping up any bits from the bottom of the pan. Let it simmer for 2 minutes.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss with the sauce. Add the shrimp back in and stir to combine.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, lemon zest, and fresh parsley.
  7. Serve with extra parsley for garnish.
